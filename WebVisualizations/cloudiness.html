Cloudiness

There is no clear relationship between cloudiness and a city's latitude. However, the chart demonstrates that at the time the data was captured, cities tended to have either very high or verylow cloudiness percentages. There were far fewer cities that reported cloudiness percentages between 20% and 60%.
